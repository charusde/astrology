Aries (March 21 - April 19)

Aries are adventurous, impulsive, and confident. They are natural-born leaders and love to take risks.

Taurus (April 20 - May 20)

Tauruses are reliable, practical, and sensuous. They value stability and security, and are known for their determination.

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)

Geminis are curious, versatile, and communicative. They are quick-witted and love to learn new things.

Cancer (June 21 - July 22)

Cancers are emotional, sensitive, and nurturing. They are deeply connected to their families and are known for their loyalty.
